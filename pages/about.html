Our Vision

Mouth-Talk was specifically designed to empower genuine movie-goers against unethical movie criticism. We've implemented a unique mechanism that categorizes audiences based on their personal taste. By using generated unique codes during ticket booking, audiences can provide weighted star reviews that are forwarded to their respective categories.
